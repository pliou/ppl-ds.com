Privacy Policy for PPL Extensions

Last updated: 30.03.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pawel Pliousnin Digital Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in relation to the following extensions and extension groups:

  • PPL Scored Search for Magento / Adobe Commerce
  • PPL Order Metrics for Magento / Adobe Commerce
  • PPL Action Widget for Magento / Adobe Commerce
  • PPL Reports for Magento / Adobe Commerce
  • PPL Developer Tools and PPL Hub modules for Magento / Adobe Commerce
  • PPL Order Comment for Magento / Adobe Commerce
  • PPL Rights Management for TYPO3

General Statement

Our extensions run inside Magento / Adobe Commerce or TYPO3 environments and do not independently collect or store personal data outside of those systems.

Magento, Adobe Commerce or TYPO3 itself may process personal data as part of normal operation. This is outside the scope of our extensions.

Information We Collect

  • We do not directly collect or store personal data.
  • Our extensions do not include tracking, analytics, or profiling functionality.
  • If third-party services are integrated by the merchant, only the minimum data required for functionality may be transmitted.

How We Use Information

Any data processed by our extensions is used exclusively for:

  • Providing core functionality
  • Improving system performance and search accuracy (Scored Search)
  • Processing and displaying order-related metrics (Order Metrics)
  • Displaying admin actions, reports, comments, development tools or delegated TYPO3 rights controls

We do not:

  • Sell personal data
  • Share data for marketing purposes
  • Use data for profiling or advertising

Data Storage

Our extensions do not store personal data outside the host system.

All data processing occurs within the Magento / Adobe Commerce or TYPO3 system and is controlled by the merchant or site operator.

Our extensions do not create independent external databases, external storage, or remote data persistence. Configuration or operational data required by an extension remains inside the host application environment.

Hosting & Server Logs

While our extensions do not collect personal data, the hosting environment, such as the web server, Magento / Adobe Commerce system or TYPO3 system, may automatically log technical data such as:

  • IP addresses
  • Request logs
  • System events

This is standard server behavior and not controlled by our extensions.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data processed within the scope of our extensions.

However, overall system security depends on the Magento / Adobe Commerce installation and hosting environment.

Third-Party Services

If the merchant integrates third-party services, those services may process data according to their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for third-party data handling.
