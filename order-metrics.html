What it does

Order Metrics adds order-based reports, CSV exports and scheduled report emails to the Magento admin.

Business reports

Revenue, profit, catalog performance, returns, cancellations, order status and marketing reports.

Comparison engine

Compare report results between periods such as month versus month, year versus year or campaign periods.

Scheduled reports

Send one or multiple reports by email with CSV attachments through cron-based schedules.

CSV export

Export report rows for spreadsheets, BI tools or internal checks.

Customer lifecycle

Customer lifetime value, cohorts, activity changes and reactivation reports.

Performance diagnostics

Benchmark report runtime, dataset size, row counts and query cost to identify heavy reports.
